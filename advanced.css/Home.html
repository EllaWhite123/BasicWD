The Hunger Games Characters

The Hunger Games is a fascinating apocalyptic fiction franchise written by Suzzane Collins in 2008. The series took off so well that each novel recieved it's own movie with a roaring fandom. Thanks to Suzanne's new addition to The Hunger Games collection, The Ballad o Songbirds and Snakes, the series is trending yet again. Not only are the themes of government control and division among the people of the country brilliantly portrayed, but the development of the fictional characters is a mesmerizing element.

From Panem to District 12, every character the reader meets along the journey of the book, or movie for that matter, is incredibly complex in their own way. Lets dive deep into the inner monolouges, personalities, up-bringings and more in some of our favorite characters from The Hunger Games.
